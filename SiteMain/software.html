What is Comfort Zone?

Comfort Zone™ is a self-service, cloud-based ergonomics solution that identifies gaps caused by work environment conditions preventing workers from performing their job duties at an optimal level.

Combined with ergonomic services, Comfort Zone maximizes worker productivity by identifying correctable conditions to reduce or eliminate the performance gap and associated health risks such as musculoskeletal disorders (MSDs) or environmental factors.

reports

The new Comfort Zone version provides users with more relevant reporting to visualize the productivity benefits of your ergonomics program. Comfort Zone offers an easy to view dashboard with a variety of management level reports which can be downloaded to a local drive for future consumption.

Customer experience

Throughout the new version of Comfort Zone, you will see a significantly improved customer experience. The updated user experience was driven by feedback received directly from our customers and industry stakeholders.

Survey

Comfort Zone’s survey offers a digitized record of assessing the risk profile of an employee. In this version of Comfort Zone, we’ve simplified the ways employees and evaluators access the survey module. This makes it easier to diagnose potential discomfort and opportunities for productivity improvement.
